About us

HIM (Human In Me) was established in 2019 as a volunteer organization mainly supported by individuals and voluntary private organizations. Our main goal is to help disadvantaged children, youth and poor women live a better life and achieve the quality of life of the intended beneficiaries.

The aim of our project is to help the most vulnerable children, youth and women who lack adequate food, access to education and better healthcare.

To transform target children, youth and women holistically after they have benefited from a self-help process and motivational programme.
